Welcome to Wordle Sports Teams!

Rules are the same as the original wordle app but the word bank is from all four major professional sports leagues in the US (NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL).

Instead of guessing words with 5 letters, each word is 6 letters and you have 5 rounds of guesses.

Hint: Both singular and plural versions of team names are possible dependent on the word fitting to 6 letters. Ex) Bulldog/Bulldogs
